Advancing Human Creativity

University of the Downtown's mission is simple: to advance human creativity. UTown believes creativity is the most essential skill for success in today’s society and has educated generations of groundbreaking artists, performers, designers and creative leaders for more than 141 years. After being granted university status in 1987, University of Downtown became the largest institution of its kind in the nation, offering programs in design, fine arts, media arts, crafts, music, dance, theater and writing. It now features 30 undergraduate arts majors, 15 graduate programs and the nation’s first PhD program in Creativity.

Is UTown right for you? Discover a welcoming community of artists, designers, media makers, performers and writers in the heart of one of the nation’s most exciting cultural centers.

Facts About UTown

As one of the nation's only comprehensive arts universities, University of Downtown offers a unique environment in which students from many disciplines—artists, performers, writers and media makers—interact in a dynamic, collaborative community of creativity. Our approximately 1,500 students are enrolled in 43 undergraduate and graduate programs on the university’s campus at the center of Philadelphia’s vibrant Avenue of the Arts. The institution’s roots as a national leader in educating creative individuals date back to our founding in 1876.
